This project is design to empower, educate, and provide for children without the limitations of borders. Our Vision: We see a world where children are free from poverty and live in happiness; this world will be one that ensures every child can receive an adequate education, and this world will be free from diseases such as HIV/AIDS and malaria. Children's Humanitarian International will be known as a global organization dedicated to empowering all children to be self-sufficient.

Who are we?
We are a creative logo designer and concept managers, we use our skills and time to create an inspiring logos and manage your concept into professional brandings. Our services varies from logo design to creative web applications. With our services we desire is to reach out to homeless, broken children out there who on a normal day might not be seen by the media houses. We try our best to ensure health security including the prevention and eradication of HIV/AIDS, malaria, and other diseases in the developing world; providing health services, immunizations, and medical supplies to areas that such services or supplies may not be readily available. Additionally, developing local programs to address child health including nutritional eating, reduction of childhood obesity, and encouraging physical activities among youth.
